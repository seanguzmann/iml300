THE THIRD CINEMA PROJECT is a digital archive & streaming platform dedicated exclusively to lost and under-distributed Afro-Latino and Black independent films from the latter half of the 20th century through the present day and into the future.

This site doubly serves as a spiritual space to honor the artists who created this lost media and were systemically erased by the mainstream industry.
