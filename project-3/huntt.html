REBECA HUNTT

Rebeca Huntt is an Afro-Latina writer and director born and raised in New York City. She wrote and directed her first feature-length film Beba, which premiered at the Toronto International Film Festival and was released by NEON in 2022.

"I WANTED TO EXPLORE THE PSYCHIC WOUNDS FAMILIES INFLICT ON ONE ANOTHER."

Race is central to Beba (2021)'s story. She carries the ancestry of her dark-skinned Dominican father and light-skinned Venezuelan mother, immigrants who met in New York. Her father rose from poverty into the working class, moving his family into “the best I could afford.”
