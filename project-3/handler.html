MARIO HANDLER

Mario Handler is a renowned Uruguayan filmmaker and photographer, widely recognized as a pioneer of Uruguayan militant cinema and a key figure in the New Latin American Cinema movement.

Handler's work primarily focuses on social and political themes, especially class struggles and the lives of marginalized individuals.
